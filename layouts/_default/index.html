Hello, I'm Ryan Bagley. I'm a network engineer, amateur writer, and hobbyist outdoor adventurer living in the American West.

I work at Goldman Sachs, which sometimes eats up a lot of my free time. But I'm part of an awesome team and work on exciting projects.

I enjoy writing and photographing my adventures in the outdoors so that people can see the beauty of this unique geographic region. There is a lot to see in the wilderness but I try to share the little snippets I have experienced.

I also write about a variety of other topics that are able to steal my attention. If you enjoy thoughtful articles and some neat pictures from a guy running around in the wilderness then check out my blog or subscribe to my RSS feed.
